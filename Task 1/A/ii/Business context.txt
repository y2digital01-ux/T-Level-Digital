Greenfield Local Hub is a cooperative organisation based around local produce, including food and drink. They have a collective marketing for local farmers and food producers, enabling them to share their products through any easy system. They currently have a system that contains product management, stock control and product traceability.
They’ve explained they want to add a digital solution to their company, allowing for customers, consumers, and producers to have an easy access and navigable system to purchase and sell products. It would essentially allow for customers to create an account, purchase a product, and actively track deliveries. They would be given an option in settings, allowing them to have access to producer features/accessibility on top of consumer features/accessibility – this is how producers and farmers gain access to producer features.

Greenfield Local Hub is a cooperative of local farmers and food producers. They offer a single location retail outlet for locally produced food and drink. As well as a collective marketing for local farmers and food producers. They also offer product management, stock control and product traceability.
GLH would like a solution that provides information about the producers, their methods and the benefits of buying locally. They would like it so that their customers can view products with transparent pricing and availability, place orders for collection or delivery, and manage their accounts and view order history. They also want to provide a dashboard to enable producers to manage stock levels update product details, and view orders.
There are features suggested by the client. These are: order management, scheduling and tracking features. A loyalty scheme that provides discounts and special offers. And finally, accessibility features to support a wide range of users.
